The Types of Bullying

9°B Students - 05/16-17/2023

★ Bullying is a very common practice in different situations, all of which cause distress and pain for the person who is being bullied. Bullying includes actions such as making threats,spreading rumors, attacking someone physically or verbally and excluding someone from a group on purpose and they usually want to make the person feel less powerful or helpless.

★Physical bullying is the use of physical actions to intimidate and gain control over a target. The physical actions are unwanted by the victim and can either cause damage to their body or possessions. It is an aggressive behavior with the intention to intimidate other people. Some actions that the bully makes is: hitting, tripping, kicking, pinching, damage of property and shoving.
